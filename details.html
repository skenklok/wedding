FAQs

Travelling from Italy or Greece

If you are traveling from Greece, or Italy, we recommend flying into one of the following airports:

  • London City Airport (LCY): The closest airport to Greenwich, about 30 minutes away by public transport.
  • Heathrow Airport (LHR): A major international hub with excellent connections to central London.
  • Gatwick Airport (LGW): Another major airport with good transport links to central London.
  • Stansted Airport (STN): Another major airport with good transport links to central London.

Getting to the Trafalgar Tavern

The Trafalgar Tavern is well-connected by various modes of transport:

  • DLR (Docklands Light Railway): The nearest DLR station is Cutty Sark for Maritime Greenwich, which is a short walk from the pub. You can easily connect to the DLR from Canary Wharf, which is on the Jubilee Line.
  • Uber Boat by Thames Clippers: For a scenic route, take the Uber Boat from central London (e.g., Westminster or North Greenwich) directly to Greenwich Pier, which is a pleasant stroll away from the tavern. More Info
  • Public Transport: Buses and trains also serve the area well, with frequent services to and from central London.
